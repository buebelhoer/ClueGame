Added Computer only play
Added logging of what rooms players are in for analysis purposes
exported to jar
added command like options to enable certain compiler options:
	DEBUG - prints soluitons to the consoles as they are created
	SIMULATE (max turns) - simulates up to (max turns) turns of a game with all computer players
	LOG (filename) - logs the room each player is in to the filename specified
	SUPERSPEED - disables delays between turns when simulating and disables popup dialogs during the game
